Understanding Air Quality Alerts

Air quality alerts are generated when AQI levels exceed safe thresholds. Different severity levels indicate the level of health concern:

High AQI above 200 - Sensitive groups should limit outdoor activities
Critical AQI above 300 - Health emergency. Avoid all outdoor activities

About BreathX

BreathX is a public urban air intelligence platform that monitors and analyzes air quality across major Indian cities. Using advanced analytics powered by Haskell, we provide accurate AQI readings, trend analysis, health recommendations, and comparative insights to help citizens make informed decisions about outdoor activities.
